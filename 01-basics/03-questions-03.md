# Questions 03
* Naam - name
* Kaw - Place
* Tham - Price
* Iska - [Ithanudaiya]
* Uska - [Athanudaiya]
* Inka - His [Ivanudaiya]
* Inke - Her [Ivaludaiya]
* Unka - His [Unnudaiya]
* Unke - Her [Avaludaiya]
* Ka - daiya [ Uska, iska, unka] - Computer ka screen - Computer's screen
* Ea - Intha [ Ea wala - Like this]
* Wo - Antha [ Wo wala - Like that]

# Examples
* Pooja, Wo laptop ka password kya hey?
* Uska password 1234 hey
* Laptop looks good, Kya tham key?
